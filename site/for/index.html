Mneme HQ is the architectural governance layer for AI-assisted development. Different roles meet that problem from different angles — CTOs and VP Engineering see review capacity collapse under AI throughput; Platform and DevEx teams own consistency across every coding agent; Staff and Principal Engineers spend their weeks repeating decisions that should already be enforced. Pick the path that matches how you spend yours.
